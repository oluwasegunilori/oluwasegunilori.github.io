Health is Wealth

Oyeleke Ibukunoluwa
27 Aug 2019

H ealth encompasses the physical,social and mental well being. It is the greatest gift and wealth given to you by God and thus, you have to guard it jealously. It is superior to the other wealth you acquire by the work of thy hands.Your health is likened to a machine which wears out after it has undergone a lot of friction without lubrication..The lubricant here is your 'Diet'. Your diet determines the nature of your health. Consume lots of veggies, enough fruits, especially those that contain antioxidants, they include orange, pineapple etc. Antioxidants prevent ageing, that is, senescence. Avoid the intake of junks, they cause a lot of damage to the health..Live healthily and enjoy the wealth you acquired by the work of thy hands.
